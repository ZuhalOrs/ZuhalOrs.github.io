Why Vote Us?

Here are some reasons why you should vote for us:

  1. We think for the people.
    • Our people are the ones that are going to bring this country forward, not the government.
  2. We believe in equality
    • Everyone is equal and evryone has to be treated the same way.
  3. Our country should be envisioned, created, governed and approved by the people.
    • We believe that the people that are affected should be the ones that have a say in where they are, to all intents and purposes, going to live
  4. Production should be geared towards use, not profit.
    • We believe that we all should have our needs met by the society we live in. the government should care more for its citizens by meeting those needs
