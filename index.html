Social Civic Party

We are a (mostly) socialist party who aims for an equal country and public.

We believe that all wealth should be owned by communities and all services should be run for the good of communities, not for private profit. We believe that people would work for the good of the community, developing empathy, sympathy and bonds of responsibility for other human beings, especially the most vulnerable. We believe that humans can be motivated by moral incentives, not just material incentives like money.
